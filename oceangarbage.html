Summary Of Project

A fully automatic garbage collector that uses AI and cameras to control the machine.

Visuals Of The Project

Brief Introduction

This ocean collection contraption is fully autonomous by using a camera which the information is then relayed to the AI to differentiate between trash and marine life. This project is solely a design project and was made for an Introduction to Engineering course.

  • Filter mediums and Filtration materials (sand, cotton, etc.)

How It Works

The machine is first placed in a body of water with any depth as it can float. The machine uses 2 rods to create a small vortex periodically to bring trash from the surface closer and once trash is seen, the AI will control a suction tube to transfer the trash to its storage tank. It also has different types of detterents to keep marine life away so that th machine can do its job with minimal risk of harm to them.

Challenges and Takeaways

Some challenges included brainstorming ways to deter marine life as that was a huge problem to solve since I wanted to implement the machine in which the habitats were not destroyed or causing harm and also marine life was not interfering with the machine. To do this with minimal harm, I resorted to known natural behviours. These include fish being sensitive to light which can be used to steer fish away. This minimizes the risk of endagering the lives of aquatic animals.

Future Improvements

To improve this project, I would need to reseach on how to make large and heavy objects float in water and would need make a design that can be implemented to the machine as that was something I had overlooked during the design process. This would make the design feel more realistic and complete.
